Rick and Morty Character Analytics

Show Overview

The series revolves around Rick Sanchez, a cynical and alcoholic mad scientist, and his easily distressed grandson Morty Smith, who frequently travel to different dimensions and planets with Rick. These adventures often lead to chaos and trouble for Morty's family, who are also often dragged along. The show explores themes of family dynamics, existentialism, and the nature of reality through its chaotic and comedic premise.

The show started in 2013, and currently has seven seasons, with an eighth season getting ready to start. Each season has approximately ten episodes.
