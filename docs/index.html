Show Overview

Rick and Morty is an American adult animated science fiction sitcom created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. It follows the misadventures of Rick Sanchez—an alcoholic, nihilistic genius scientist—and his good-hearted but easily flustered grandson Morty Smith as they traipse through parallel dimensions, alien worlds, and bizarre “what-if” scenarios. The show premiered December 2, 2013 on Cartoon Network’s late-night block Adult Swim and remains one of its flagship series; episodes are also available to stream on Hulu and Max in the U.S.

As of December 17, 2023, Rick and Morty spans seven seasons totaling 71 episodes, with an eighth season scheduled to debut May 25, 2025. The core family of characters includes:

  • Rick Sanchez
  • Morty Smith
  • Summer Smith
  • Beth Smith
  • Jerry Smith
